About

Cooper Union Data Science Club, a student organized club at The Cooper Union , seeks to disseminate the theories and methods of data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

What We Do

Speaker
Series

We invite researchers and game-changers in the field of Machine Learning and its applications to speak about their work to students at the Cooper Union in order to bring context into what they're learning.

Networking With
Professionals

We provide our club members with an established network of employers seeking to hire individuals with experience in the field of Applied Machine Learning.

Officers

Cooper Union Data Science Club reflects the culture of The Cooper Union, which was founded by Peter Cooper with the intent to provide accessible education in the field of design to people of all socioeconomic backgrounds. The DSC is committed to expanding the skillsets of its members to compete in a global marketplace.
